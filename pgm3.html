About Us

MEA Engineering College, Perinthalmanna is a self‑financing engineering institution in Malappuram district, Kerala, known for its commitment to value‑based and quality technical education. Located at Nellikunnu, near Perinthalmanna, the campus is spread across a scenic area overlooking hills and valleys.Governed by the Muslim Educational Association (MEA), under the Samastha Kerala Jammiyyathul Ulama, the college is affiliated with the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and approved by the AICTE.Apart from academic facilities, MEA Engineering College provides hostel facilities, a well‑stocked library, laboratories, sports amenities, and auditoriums. The institution also strives to create an environment that fosters holistic development, professionalism, integrity, and social responsibility among its students.

Courses Offered

The college offers a number of undergraduate engineering programmes in areas such as Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Computer Science, Information Technology, and emerging domains like Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. It also has postgraduate courses and puts emphasis on research, infrastructure, and employability.
