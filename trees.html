Trees are among the most important and valuable living organisms on Earth. They provide oxygen, improve air quality, conserve water, preserve soil, and support wildlife. With their strong trunks and wide canopies, trees offer shade, reduce noise pollution, and help lower temperatures in urban areas. Beyond their environmental benefits, trees also supply us with fruits, wood, medicine, and countless other resources. From towering oaks and pines to flowering cherry and mango trees, they come in many varieties and are found in forests, parks, gardens, and streetscapes. Trees play a crucial role in maintaining ecological balance and are essential for the health and survival of all living beings.
